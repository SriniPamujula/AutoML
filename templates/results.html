Understanding the Metrics:

Accuracy

% of all predictions correct. Best for balanced datasets.

Precision

Of positive predictions, how many are actually positive. Matters when false positives are costly.

Recall

Of actual positives, how many were caught. Matters when missing positives is costly.

F1

Balance of precision & recall. Best for imbalanced datasets.

{% else %}

Understanding the Metrics:

R²

How much variance the model explains. 1.0 = perfect, 0 = no better than mean.

MAE

Mean Absolute Error. Average size of prediction errors in original units.

RMSE

Root Mean Squared Error. Penalizes large errors more heavily than MAE.
