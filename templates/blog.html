Guide to Daily e-inpatient list

March 21, 2017 by Srinivasa Pamujula

The e-inpatient list has various patient details such as care manager, admission, discharge and expected dates, primary insurance, service provider, care coordinator and reason for admission. It also identifies the international patient status. This report integrates Epic and Salesforce CRM details on daily basis.

This blog post shows a few different ways of accessing Daily e-inpatient list.

  • JHI Analytics Website
  • JHI Reporting Portal
  • Daily Census Link

The e-inpatient report lists only Inhouse, Discharged, Pre-admit inpatients.

JHI Analytics Website

The report can be accessed through JHI Analytics web site https://analyticsjhi.jhmi.edu >> Links >> Daily Census. The screen shot is shown below.


The report will be presented as shown below.


Saving the report

To save the report to PDF or Excel, click floppy icon and click on PDF or Excel.


Printing the report

To print the report, click the printer icon and follow the print dialogue.


JHI Reporting Portal

If you have access to multiple JHI Analytics reports you may want to use the JHI Reporting portal at https://analyticsjhi.jhmi.edu/reports (use your JHEDID when prompted). The reporting portal will be presented. Click on Care Management folder



Click on the Admit Discharge Census report to view the report.


Making Favorite Reports

Click on the ellipsis (three dots) and select Add to Favorites These favorite reports will be presented as soon as logging to the portal.
